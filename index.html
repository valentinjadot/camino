Reserve "la Estancia"

In the 19th century, approximately in 1870, the first house of the “estancia” belonging to the Gálvez family was built here, the first house of what was later called the town of Sauce Viejo.

The reserve of “la Estancia” revalues the local history, the importance of the native forest and the species that inhabit it. It is an environment full of nature, biodiversity and balanced ecosystem, part of the pre-delta of the Paraná River - considered one of the largest in the world - on the banks of the Coronda River.

We live in an environment that brims abundance and natural diversity. In the reserve, you will find ‘ombúes’ (phytolacca dioica) of more than 200 years called “gaucho tree” or “beautiful shadow”. Despite its large size, it is not an actual tree : it is considered a giant grass.

We invite you to take a rest next to the flowers of seibo (Erytrina crista-galli) our national flower or eat blackberries in the forest (Morus alba and nigra), admire the the nest - beautiful creation of mud and straw - of the Hornero (Furnarius Rufus) our national bird, see in action the Kingfisher (Alcedo Atthis) and how hundreds of species of birds, land and water plants, insects, reptiles, mammals of the islands such as capybaras and otters interact in a fascinating river landscape.

Exploring this nature is for us a spiritual experience. You will understand why Darwin came here.

From this point - Coronda - to Santa Fe, the road is not very safe. From here to the north, the west bank of the Parana ceases to be inhabited; consequently, sometimes the Indians arrive there and attack the travelers. The nature of the landscape also favors this, because instead of a grassy plain there is a very open forest composed of low and thorny mimosa
- October 1833, Charles Darwin

Projects

The camping and hostel projects are joining their forces to live and share the power of nature and its magic, the strength of the river, the peace of the forest.

In 2016, Marcelo starts to live in the ranch and and recover the spaces by starting the campsite. In February of 2018 Alejandro joins the project with the purpose of transforming one of the houses into a hostel. We lived then the first winter together, between talks, work, the cold and cooking with friends and our first volunteer, the little Isis.

Today we are receiving volunteers from the town and from all over the world to help us with their creativity, their art, their knowledge and a wonderful energy that allows us to continue this journey and enjoy the first summer season together.

Our intention is to promote the importance of the native forest in the ecosystem and in the human life, to co-create a space of cultural exchange and celebration. This leads us towards the conscious and sustainable consumption of resources and energy, to develop the various productions with the agroecological techniques of permaculture. It is also part of the planning to restore and revalue the big house "casco de estancia" as a local historical heritage.

The place guides us on the way.

Experience

Each night is unique around the fire: a natural encounter in the garden of the hostel where the music, food and laughter are lost in a sea of stars. When the sky allows it, we celebrate the full moon as a manifestation of another cycle, a phase of life that becomes history and a new one that begins. And then the night becomes another day.

Here you can reconnect with yourself and the environment, recharge your energy, lose yourself to find yourself. You can learn and/or teach natural openfire cooking, typical dishes or just enjoy some mate on the riverbank. We guide you through the countryside so that you know each place and identify the predominant fauna and flora, interact with the horses or dive into the forest.

You can also hike through the town, visit the museum, a local exquisite local milk factory with sales all over the world, taste the fish cuisine for what is known as Sauce Viejo. In the main square of the village you can eat delicious pizzas and enjoy a cultural space that offers live music on weekends.

Here the Folkloric Festival of the Sinner takes place in January, the most important in the province that dresses the town as a fiesta and we are visited by consecrated artists from all over the country. In addition, the town of Sauce Viejo has an itinerary of events and popular festivals for the whole year.

In the hostel we propose various activities such as art and craft workshops, canoe trips, boat trips around the islands, night excursions to the island, fishing trips, among others.
